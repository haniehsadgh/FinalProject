Game Instruction

×
  • To Start, Click on 'Roll Dice' button
  • You can roll your dice up tp 3 times
  • After 3 rounds, the game is over and the final result is shown on the screen
  • If you want to play more, click on 'New Game' button

Rules for scoring:

  • Your score is the total value of the 2 dices
  • However, if any of your dice gets 1, you get 0 score on that round
  • Bonus point, if you roll a pair of the same number, you get double score from that number
